Welcome to Mekatrice Diagnostic Limited

Mekatrice Diagnostics Limited is one of the leading "all encompassing" medical diagnosis company operating out of Lagos, Nigeria. Mekatrice is registered and regulated in Nigeria to carry out functions as a full service diagnostic center providing bespoke health solutions to patients, The company began operations in May 2009 and has expanded its footprint into two regional branches within its first year of operations.

The founder - Dr Kingsley Oboti - and his team which includes consultant radiologist, sonologist, radiographers, medical lab scientist and health managers and health managers who have gamered decades of relevant experience in their respective fields. Mekatrice has proposed a partnership with Newham University Hospital East London to Support the provision of high quality world class diagnostic services to Africa.

Its diagnostic services include the following: CT Scan, Ultrasound Scan, X-Ray, ECG, Hormonal Assay, Full Laboratory Services, Mammogram and a Full Ambulance Service
